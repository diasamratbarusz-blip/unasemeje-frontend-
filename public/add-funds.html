Important: Turn Off Flag for Review

To ensure your Instagram followers are delivered properly, you MUST disable the "Flag for Review" feature.

Step-by-Step Instructions:

1
Go to Settings
Open Instagram → Tap your profile → Tap ☰ (menu) → Settings and privacy
2
Find Follow Settings
Scroll down and tap "Follow and invite friends"
3
Disable Flag for Review
Under "Followers" section, find "Flag for review" and turn it OFF
4
Confirm
Once disabled, you can safely order Instagram followers.
