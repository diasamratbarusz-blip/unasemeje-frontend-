Important: Turn Off Flag for Review

To ensure your Instagram followers are delivered properly, you MUST disable the "Flag for Review" feature in your Instagram settings.

Step-by-Step Instructions:

1 Go to Settings
Open Instagram → Tap your profile → Tap ☰ (menu) → Settings and privacy
2 Find Follow Settings
Scroll down and tap "Follow and invite friends"
3 Disable Flag for Review
Under "Followers" section, find "Flag for review" and turn it OFF (toggle should be gray, not blue)
4 Confirm
Once disabled, you can safely order Instagram followers and they will be delivered automatically.

Why This is Important:

When "Flag for Review" is ON, Instagram requires your approval for new followers. This means our automated delivery system won't work, and your followers won't be added to your account.

💡 Pro Tip: After disabling this setting, wait 5-10 minutes before placing your order to ensure the change takes effect.
